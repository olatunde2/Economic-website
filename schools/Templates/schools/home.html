About UTH International


UTH International School, is a private mixed primary school located in Tunde Division of City Council of Olatunde. It is a day school providing a local curriculum UTH International school is on an International level but follows a local curriculum. It's situated along Oke bypass and has an enrollment of over 3500 pupils with 300 well-trained teachers and over 200 non-teaching staff members. The pupils come from all over the world. The school has been and will continue to be an academic hub of Ola because of the balanced curriculum offered. We are a multi-cultural institution that provides awell-rounded balanced enducation to children from different backgrounds

The school mission is "to facillitate first-class education and civillsation to children with and from oustide OLA with the hope that there will be a beter world community tomorrow".
